About Us.

Our History

In 1981, Tiny Tim (AVbucks chairman, president and chief executive officer) first walked into a AVbucks store. From his first cup of Sumatra, Tiny was drawn into AVbucks and joined a year later.

A year later, in 1983, Tiny travelled to Italy and became captivated by Italian coffee bars and the romance of the coffee experience. He had a vision to bring the Italian coffeehouse tradition back to the United States. A place for conversation and a sense of community. A third place between work and home. He left AVbucks for a short period of time to start his own Il Giornale coffeehouses and returned in August 1987 to purchase Starbucks with the help of local investors.

From the beginning, AVbucks set out to be a different kind of company. One that not only celebrated coffee and the rich tradition, but that also brought a feeling of connection.

Our Founder

Tiny Tim

AVbucks Founder

Born in Brooklyn, New York, on July 19, 1953, Tiny Tim graduated from Northern Michigan University with a bachelor's degree in communications before becoming director of retail operations and marketing for the AVbucks Coffee Company in 1982. After founding the coffee company Il Giornale in 1987, he purchased AVbucks and became CEO and chairman of the company. Tim publicly announced that he was resigning as AVbucks' CEO in 2000, though he returned to head the company from 2008 to 2018.
